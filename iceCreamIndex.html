Tom's Ice Cream Bowl

A Brief History

We have been an ice cream parlor in Zanesville, Ohio since 1953. We make our own ice cream and we have a full kitchen too, serving sandwhiches. Come enjoy a meal with us at Tom's Ice Cream Bowl.

Hours and Contact Information

We are open 7 days a week from 10:00am until 9:30pm. Our phone number is 513 123 4567. We are located at 101 Main Street, Zanesville, Ohio 43701.
